Classes

With six distinct classes, players can build their soldier to match their play-style, or the needs of their squad, outfit or Empire. Each combat role, vehicle and weapon has extensive skill trees that will allow players to grow characters, unlock weapons, attachments, equipment, specialized skills, vehicles, and more. The six classes are light assault, combat medic, heavy assault, engineer, infiltrator and max.

Weapon

In Plantside 2 beside the common weapon each faction have their own featured weapon, each of them have their have different specialty and performance

Terran Republic

Chain Blade Knife

Terran Republic’s Chainblade uses centuries-old technology in an extremely compact package. Though initially provided for utility purposes, the Chainblade’s synthetic diamond teeth have proven extremely effective in close quarters combat.

FAS - 20 Barrage

The fully automatic FAS-20 “Barrage” shotgun features an extremely high rate of fire, making it one of the most fearsome weapons available for close-quarters combat.

MSW - 2 Rampage

Assembled with lightweight polymers and alloys, the Rampage was developed for infantry who favor mobility above all else.

T20 - F

The bolt-action T20-F sniper rifle utilizes the devastating 11x105 cartridge, the most powerful round used by the TR. Its unrivaled stopping power is only kept in check by its slow rate of fire.

T1 - Cycler Assault Rifle

The T1 Cycler assault rifle possesses a good balance of damage output, range, and accuracy. Its high rate of fire and large magazine capacity allows soldiers to drop targets at medium range with ease.

TX1 Repeater Pistol

The iconic TX1 Repeater is the standard TR sidearm. Its 3-round burstfire configuration makes it extremely deadly in close quarters.

New Conglomerate

NC14 Bolt Driver

The NC14 Bolt Driver uses an electromagnetic propulsion system to drive an extremely accurate, high velocity projectile at distant targets. It is prized by New Conglomerate snipers for its stopping power over long ranges and has become the primary weapon of choice for many soldiers entering the field of battle.

NC1 Gauss Rife

The standard issue NC1 Gauss Rifle was the first successful implementation of handheld coilgun weaponry. It owes its high accuracy and lethality to the high velocity metallic slugs it uses.

NC4 Mag - Shot

Reliable and accurate, the NC4 Mag-Shot features a compact electromagnetic propulsion system that provides the New Conglomerate’s standard issue sidearm considerable stopping power.

Stiletto EM9

The powerful Stiletto EM9 was released shortly before Auraxis' global war yet still remains in common use due to its famed reliability and high stopping power.

EMC7 Impetus

The semi-automatic EMC7 Impetus sniper rifle features a high rate of fire with a significant punch, allowing marksman to quickly and effectively eliminate targets from long range.

Reaper DMR

The Reaper DMR's large ferromagnetic projectiles excel at dropping targets from afar. While it possesses incredible power shot-for-shot, its low rate of fire makes it ineffective in close quarters.

Vanu Sovereignty

Orion VS54

The Orion is the standard issue LMG for Vanu Heavy Assault soldiers. Its lightweight construction allows it to fire accurately even from the hip.

Pulsar VS1 Assault Rifle

The Pulsar Compact VS5 is the most widely used carbine in the Vanu Sovereignty. It features minimal recoil to give it a significant advantage when firing at distant targets.

Beamer VS3 Pistol

The Beamer is the Vanu Sovereignty’s standard-issue sidearm. It fires accurate laser projectiles that deliver considerable stopping power without sacrificing rate of fire.

VX25

The Vanu Sovereignty’s experimental Pandora VX25's semi-automatic plasma shotgun is devastating in close quarters.

Artemis

The Artemis VX26 scout rifle features an energy efficient receiver, allowing for longer sustained fire between new power cores.

Polaris

Embedded recoil dampeners along the barrel of the Polaris VX29 give it an extended range over other LMGs. Its low recoil and high-capacity power cores make it an excellent choice for outdoor combat situations.

Vehicals

In Plantside 2 there are 6 common vehicles and 2 of the faction featured vehicle, each of them have their have different specialty and performance

Common Vehicles

Flash

The nimble Flash ATV’s interchangeable weapon system and offroad abilities allow it to make surprise movements. Its low cost and maneuverability have made it a popular choice amongst infantry.

Sunderer

The imposing Sunderer ground transport can hold a dozen soldiers and sports a pair of swappable roof-mounted weapons. This heavily armored behemoth is capable of simply driving through defended front lines to reach objectives.

Colossus

The Colossus is a heavy tank armed with a dynamic Mammoth Cannon primary weapon and four secondary weapons for defense. In addition, the Colossus can deploy into an anti-air skylance battery.

Lightning

The versatile Lightning from Nanite Systems can quickly navigate rough terrain for strategic flanking attacks. It also features a highly interchangeable weapon system that allows it to adapt to nearly any ground or air threat.

Liberator

Nanite Systems’ Liberator features a 3-man crew and unsurpassed air-to-ground firepower. The heavily armed gunship features an accurate belly-mounted cannon that can decimate ground vehicles and infantry alike, as well as nose and tail mounted autocannons to tear apart enemy aircraft.

Galaxy

The massive Galaxy dropship can carry and air-drop up to a dozen soldiers onto objectives where a ground assault would be impossible. The Galaxy also comes equipped with four passenger-operated 20mm machineguns and functions as a mobile infantry spawn point while on the ground.

Terran Republic

Mosquito

The swift Mosquito is by far Auraxis’ fastest vehicle. Its untouchable top speed allows it to shake enemies in dogfights, as well as perform devastating air-to-ground assaults before the enemy can react.

Prowler

The Prowler’s high top speed and maneuverability allow it to run circles around other tanks in its class. It features powerful twin 120mm cannons and a passenger-operated 20mm machine gun.

New Conglomerate

Reaver

The New Conglomerate’s Reaver possesses the heaviest armor and hardest hitting weaponry out of all of Auraxis’ interceptor aircraft. This heavy aircraft can execute and survive dangerous strafing runs that leave only burning wreckage behind.

Vanguard

By far the heaviest ground vehicle on Auraxis, the mighty Vanguard boasts an unparalleled 150mm cannon that can punch through the thickest of vehicle plating. It also features a passenger-operated 20mm machinegun for dealing with nearby infantry.

Vanu Sovereignty

Scythe

The Scythe’s incredible agility allows it to perform aerial feats that put other aircraft to shame. Equipped with powerful plasma-based weaponry and the ability to effortlessly pull off loops and barrel rolls, a good Scythe pilot is a formidable opponent.

Magrider

The levitating Magrider tank’s thrust engines allow it to move dodge projectiles laterally and drive over low obstacles. The forward-facing Plasma Cannon can melt through enemy armor, and the passenger-operated 20mm machinegun can quickly dispatch aircraft and infantry.
