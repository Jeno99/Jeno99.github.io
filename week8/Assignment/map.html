There are five warzone avaible to play in Planetside 2 there are Amerish, Hossin, Indar, Esamir and Oshur

Amerish

Amerish is a warzone continent on Auraxis. Amerish is a lush and mountainous continent, civilized yet still alive with flora, given life by plentiful water streams.


Amerish's lush groves and rocky outcroppings provide ample cover between its rolling plains and mountain passes.


Hossin

Hossin is a warzone continent on Auraxis. Hossin is a vast swamp featuring exaggerated fauna and tall hills, the skyline filled with trees hundred of meters high.


Hossin is a dense continent in every sense of the word. Featuring a large number of facilities packed close together, and an abundance of fauna across the continent, the landscape is designed to feel claustrophobic, particularly to pilots and drivers.


A thick fog also permeates the continent at most times of day, severely hampering visibility out to long ranges, further compounded on at night time.


Esamir

Esamir is a warzone continent on Auraxis. Esamir is an icy tundra with wide barren wastelands divided by frozen rivers, and violet crystals erupting from the snow to color an otherwise lifeless landscape.


Though formerly a continent devoid of plantlife, energies released by the calamity that lead to The Shattered Warpgate have caused a resurgence of life in the northeast of the tundra, and the warpgates' endless stream of skybound energy dominates the skyline for miles around.


Due to its polar location on Auraxis, Esamir does not feature the same full day-night cycle of other continents, with light levels dipping only as low as twilight before sunrise returns once more. Another feature of the continent's arctic nature is the occasional presence of an aurora in the skies above.


Indar

Indar is a warzone continent on Auraxis, Indar is an arid landmass centered on a dried up seabed, with the southern half of the continent divided between fair greenery and a rocky canyon.


Indar is a relatively high activity continent. Most of its most important facilities are centered near the divisions between each biome, which make up the iconic "Indar T", and the continents sprawling roadways and diverse biomes allow for varied combat styles across the continent.


Oshur

Oshur is an warzone continent planned on Auraxis. The fifth major continent made available, Oshur is described as a "tropical paradise brimming with research opportunities...evacuated as the Auraxian War began."


Oshur is the pioneer of a fully-featured water system, allowing all domains of combat on Auraxis to interact with the oceans in an engaging way—infantry and tracked vehicles traverse the sea bed, wheeled and hovering vehicles skirt the waves, and aircraft dart between the surface and underwater at will—albeit at great cost to their maneuverability when underwater.
