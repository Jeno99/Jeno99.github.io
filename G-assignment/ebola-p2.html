Ebola Symptoms

Symptoms may appear anywhere from 2 to 21 days after contact with the virus, with an average of 8 to 10 days. A person becomes sicker when experiencing from “dry” symtopms to “wet” symptoms.

Primary signs and symptoms of Ebola are:


Headache


Vomit


Cough


Diarrhea


Rash


Red Eyes


Fever
